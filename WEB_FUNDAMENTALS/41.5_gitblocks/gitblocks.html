What is Git?

Git is a version control system; you an think of it as a series of snapshots(commits) of your code base. With the creation of each snapsho you build out a whole application, seeing the growth of a full app. During this time you can make branches to experiment and come back to a previous snapshot you took or merge those experient into your app.
